Lights and Teapots

Stage 4 Advanced Graphics for Games Coursework

Technology Used

  • C++
  • OpenGL
  • GLSL
  • RenderDoc
    •

    This was my first time attempting advanced graphics technique such as real time lighting, shadow mapping, and reflections.
    I utilised cubemaps for omnidirectional shadows from multiple light sources, as well as for environment mapped reflections.
    I implemented a deferred rendering solution for faster lighting calculations.
    A scene graph was used for managing the movement of objects in the scene.
    I also experimented with a few post-processing effects, including chromatic abberation and gaussian blur.
